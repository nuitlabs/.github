## About Nuit

Nuit is a premium web development maison operating at the intersection of **security**, **design**, and **engineering**. We commission, architect, and deliver bespoke digital experiences — transforming static, forgettable websites into high-performance, visually commanding platforms.

We approach every project the way a French maison approaches craft: with precision, intention, and an uncompromising standard. The result is a web presence that doesn't just look exceptional — it *performs* exceptionally, built on enterprise-grade security practices from the ground up.

> *"We are turning the boring into stunning."*

***

## What We Do

Nuit operates across three disciplines, delivered as a unified service:

| Discipline | Focus |
|---|---|
| **Web Engineering** | Full-stack development with modern frameworks — Next.js, Astro, TypeScript, edge computing via Cloudflare Workers |
| **Security Architecture** | Hardened infrastructure, best practices security posture built into every layer of the stack |
| **Digital Design** | Premium UI/UX direction — motion, layout, typography, and visual identity that commands attention |

We serve both **B2B** clients seeking to elevate their digital presence and **B2C** brands requiring a direct-to-consumer experience worthy of their product.

***

## Our Standards

Nuit doesn't ship ordinary work. Every project is held to a set of non-negotiable principles:

- **Security-first** — vulnerabilities are architectural failures; we treat them as such
- **Performance by default** — edge-deployed, globally fast, no excuses
- **Visually uncompromising** — aesthetic quality is not an afterthought; it's the brief
- **Brand coherence** — every pixel, interaction, and line of copy reflects a singular identity

***

## Technology Stack

Our core stack reflects a preference for reliability, speed, and developer control:
